* == --- Equal to
* != --- doesn't Equal
* > --- Greater than
* < --- Less than
* >= --- Greater than or equal to
* <= --- Less than or equal to
* or --- Condition A or Conditon B
* and --- Condition A and Condition B
* contains --- contains tests for the presence of a substring within a string 
* startswith --- tests whether a string starts with a given substring
* endswith --- tests wheteher a string ends with a given substring
